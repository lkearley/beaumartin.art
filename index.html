I Am Beau

I'm an aerospace engineering graduate from the Georgia Institute of Technology focused on the intersection of art and engineering. Through roles in industry and through working as a makerspace mentor while at Georgia Tech, I've been able to hone my craft in manufacturing and machatronics. Artistically, my photography has appeared in the Museum of Design Atlanta, the Dogwood Arts Festival, and the High Museum of Art. I've had the great opportunity to work under several influential innovators, including renowned inventor Lonnie Johnson, and stereoscopic photographer Peter Bahouth.

Projects

Heron

The Heron was a project for the City of Decatur made of aluminum tubing, Dacron fabric, and a steel superstructure. Solar panels power the wings up and down.

Color Slug

Color Slug is a climbing robot that paints walls. A fan suctions the robot onto a surface, and a can of spray paint lays down a trail.

Dog

The Dog was painted on a sheet of reclaimed concrete board. The dog is stenciled with a freehanded background done by Zoe Genet.
